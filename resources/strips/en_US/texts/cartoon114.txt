Text: A few months ago, Jen salvaged an old [VIC-20](http://web.archive.org/web/20091015095242/http://www.kdef.com/geek/vic/) from her parent's house, adding it to our growing computer collection. It is amazing how limited the machine is- 22x23 character display, 3.5 KB of user memory- and yet, at the time, it [ruled](http://web.archive.org/web/20091015095242/http://www.geocities.com/TimesSquare/Alley/3583/Vgrabs.html) the world of multimedia home computers!
