Text: Acronyms-o-plenty!
Text: Every time a new concept arises in the computing world, a new acronym is born. It seems every 3 or 4 letter combination already means something, and the "acronymization" of our language keeps getting more [out of control](http://web.archive.org/web/20091015135159/http://www.dictionary.com/search?q=pcmcia).
