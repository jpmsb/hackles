Text: Where did the penguins go?
Text: These penguins just aren't happy unless they're sitting in front of a computer. This strip originally had a different punchline, which seemed funny when we thought it up. But when Jen finished putting the strip together, we both looked at it and realized how lame it really was. After trying in vain to come up with a better ending, we just decided to have Hackles say "Crazy penguins," and be done with it.
Text: On the plus side, we both got a kick out of the "Corn on a Stick" idea. They should really sell that stuff, mmm...
