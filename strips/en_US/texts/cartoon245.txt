Text: Invasion of the consumer electronics mega-store!
Text: At some point, well before Halloween, we start seeing a glut of Best Buy and Circuit City commercials, vying for our Christmas spending money. We admit these stores *are* pretty fun, but it's a little insidious how their commercials try to stretch the Christmas season over three months, and train us to spend! spend!! spend!!! during all of it.
